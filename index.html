Our Path

Three chapters. One finish.

The run moved from District 16, through Texas East Section 3, and into the state tournament. Each step demanded another answer. The boys kept answering.

Chapter 01

District Championship

After an early setback, Post Oak won six straight elimination games and finished the district run with a winner-take-all shutout.

Chapter 02

Section Championship

Four games. Four wins. Post Oak came through Section 3 undefeated and earned the right to play for Texas East.

Chapter 03

Texas East State Championship

Post Oak went 3–0 at state, allowed one total run, and closed the tournament with a 4–0 championship victory over Lamar.
